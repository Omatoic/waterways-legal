Request Data Deletion – WaterWays

If you would like to delete your WaterWays account and associated data, you can request this by contacting us.

What will be deleted

  • Your account (email address)
  • All saved routes and trips
  • All associated data stored in our systems

How to request deletion

Please send an email to:

omatoic@gmail.com

Include:

  • Your account email address
  • Subject: "Data Deletion Request"

Processing time

We will process your request within a reasonable timeframe (typically within 7 days).
